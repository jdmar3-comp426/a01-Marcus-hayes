The Aesir

The Aesir were one of the two primary tribes of deities in Pre-Christian Norse mythology. The Aesir included the greater of the Norse gods who resided in Asgard (one of the nine worlds within the branches of the world-tree, Yggdrasil) and possessed connections to the celestial plane. The Aesir included Odin, Thor, Frigg, Tyr, Loki, Baldur, Heimdall, Idun, and Bragi.
